Privacy Policy for Clean Game Turbo

Last updated: July 10, 2026

Clean Game Turbo ("the App", "we", "us") is developed by Kamal Tech ("Developer", "we"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use the Clean Game Turbo app (package name: com.cleangameturbo.pro) on your Android device.

By downloading, installing, or using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. Quick Summary

  • Clean Game Turbo is a cleaner and game booster app that operates locally on your device.
  • Most data processing (file scanning, RAM, cache, notifications) is performed entirely on your device and is not sent to our servers.
  • We do not have an account/login system, so we do not collect your name, email, or phone number.
  • We use third-party services (Google AdMob) to display ads, and Google Play Billing for in-app purchases (VIP subscription).

2. Information We Collect and Permissions Used

The App requests a number of Android permissions so its features can function. Below is an explanation of each permission, the data accessed, and its purpose:

Permission / FeatureData AccessedPurpose
Media Access (Photos, Videos, Audio)
READ_MEDIA_IMAGES, READ_MEDIA_VIDEO, READ_MEDIA_AUDIO, READ_MEDIA_VISUAL_USER_SELECTED, READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE		 Media file metadata and content (size, date, thumbnails) to scan for junk files, cache, and duplicate or blurry photos/videos. To display storage-cleaner scan results and the duplicate photo/video finder feature. Scanning and deletion are performed entirely on-device; media files are never uploaded to any server.
App Usage Statistics
PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS		 Information about which app is currently running in the foreground. To detect when a game starts/stops so the App can automatically apply per-game performance settings (Game Mode) and show the booster overlay. This data is processed locally and never leaves your device.
Installed App List
(<queries> for launcher apps)		 List of apps that can be launched (have a launcher icon). To show a list of games/apps in the "Add Games" and "Focus Mode" pickers so users can choose which apps to optimize or temporarily block. Does not use the QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES permission.
Notifications (Notification Listener)
BIND_NOTIFICATION_LISTENER_SERVICE, POST_NOTIFICATIONS, ACCESS_NOTIFICATION_POLICY		 Incoming notifications from other apps on your device (sender app name; notification content is readable by this system service). Used to power automatic "Do Not Disturb" mode and notification filtering/muting while a game is running (Game Mode/Focus Mode), so gameplay isn't interrupted. Processing is entirely local; notification content is never stored permanently, never logged, and never sent to our servers or any third party.
Modify System Settings
WRITE_SETTINGS		 System settings such as screen brightness. To automatically adjust screen brightness while Game Mode is active.
Display Over Other Apps
SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW		 No personal data; display permission only. To show the game booster overlay (FPS counter, quick-action buttons, custom crosshair) on top of the game app while in use.
Foreground Services
FOREGROUND_SERVICE, FOREGROUND_SERVICE_SPECIAL_USE, FOREGROUND_SERVICE_DATA_SYNC, WAKE_LOCK		 No personal data. To keep game-monitoring services (GameService, GameModeService) running in the background during a play session, as required by Android policy for long-running services.
Local VPN (Focus Mode)
BIND_VPN_SERVICE		 List of apps (e.g. chat apps) the user selects to block from internet access. The "Focus Mode" feature uses a local-only VPN to temporarily block internet access for selected apps (e.g. chat apps) so your online status/notifications stay hidden while gaming. No traffic ever leaves your device, is routed to our servers, or is shared with any third party. This is not a VPN for changing your location or masking your IP on the internet.
Network Information
ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE, ACCESS_WIFI_STATE, INTERNET		 Connection status (connected/not, network type). Required to load ads, verify VIP purchases, and ensure online features work correctly (e.g. Google Play Billing license validation).

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or any account registration information.
  • We do not access contacts, call history, SMS, location (GPS), microphone, or camera.
  • We do not store copies of your photos, videos, or notification content on our servers — everything is processed on your device.

4. How We Use Information

  • To run core features: RAM cleaning, junk/cache file cleaning, duplicate photo & video detection, game booster, and Focus Mode.
  • To display ads that fund the free version of the App.
  • To process VIP/premium purchases through Google Play Billing.
  • To improve App stability and performance through standard crash reporting from Google Play (if enabled by the Android system).

5. Data Storage and Security

App preference data (e.g. favorite games list, Game Mode settings, VIP subscription status) is stored locally on your device using local storage (Android DataStore/preferences) and is not sent to our servers, since this App has no backend server. We rely on standard Android security practices (runtime permissions, app sandboxing) to help protect data on your device.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data may only be shared with:

  • Third-party service providers listed in Section 3 (Google AdMob, Google Play Billing), for their respective functions.
  • Legal authorities, if required by applicable law.

7. Your Rights and Choices

  • Revoke permissions: You can revoke any permission (media, notifications, usage access, etc.) at any time via Android Settings > Apps > Clean Game Turbo > Permissions. Revoking certain permissions may disable some features.
  • Disable ad personalization: You can reset your ad preferences via your device's Google Settings (Google > Ads) or through the consent form (UMP) shown by the App.
  • Delete data: Uninstalling the App will remove all local data stored by the App on your device.
  • Notification Listener: Access for the notification listener service can be revoked at any time via Android Settings > Special App Access > Notification Access.

8. Children's Privacy

This App is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your region), and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so it can be removed.

9. International Users (GDPR/CCPA)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the UK, or California, you may have additional rights over your personal data (access, correction, deletion, portability, objection to processing) under GDPR or CCPA. Because this App fundamentally processes data locally and has no account system, most of these rights are relevant to data processed by our advertising partners (see Section 3). You may contact us using the information in Section 11 with any questions about these rights.
