Introduction

Clean Game Turbo respects your privacy. This application is designed to improve your gaming experience through game optimization features and local device utilities. Clean Game Turbo does not require user registration and does not collect personal information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect or store:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Passwords
  • Contacts
  • Messages
  • Photos
  • Videos
  • Audio Files
  • GPS Location
  • Payment Information

Permissions Used

Photos, Videos & Audio

Used only for:

  • Storage Analyzer
  • Photo Cleaner
  • Video Cleaner
  • Audio Cleaner

Media files never leave your device.


Accessibility Service

Accessibility is optional and is only used after you enable it. It is required for:

  • Detect Game Launch
  • Automatically Enable Game Mode
  • Automatically Disable Game Mode

Accessibility information is never collected or transmitted.


Display Over Other Apps

Overlay permission is used to display the floating Game Panel while gaming. Features include:

  • Crosshair
  • Lock Touch
  • Focus Mode
  • Silent Notifications
  • Ghost Mode
  • Screen Awake
  • Brightness Control
  • Volume Control

Notification Access

Used only to reduce interruptions while gaming. Notification content is never stored.


Do Not Disturb

Allows the application to enable Android Do Not Disturb during gameplay.


Ghost Mode (VPN)

Ghost Mode uses Android VpnService only to temporarily restrict internet access for selected chat applications. We do NOT:

  • Monitor Internet Traffic
  • Collect Browsing History
  • Collect Network Activity
  • Transmit VPN Data

Modify System Settings

Used only for Brightness Boost and restoring previous brightness.


Foreground Service

Used to keep Game Mode running while you play.

Advertising

Clean Game Turbo uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google may collect advertising identifiers and device information according to its own Privacy Policy.

Google Privacy Policy

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

Data Security

All optimization, storage analysis, and gaming features are processed locally on your device whenever possible.

Children's Privacy

Clean Game Turbo is not directed toward children under 13 years of age.

Your Rights

You may revoke any permission granted to Clean Game Turbo at any time through Android Settings.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be published on this page.
