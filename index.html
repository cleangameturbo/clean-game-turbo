Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

Thank you for using Clean Game Turbo. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information our application uses, what permissions are requested, and how your data is handled.

Information We Collect

Clean Game Turbo does not collect or store personal information such as:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Contacts
  • Photos
  • Videos
  • Messages
  • Location

The application does not require user registration or create online accounts.

Permissions Used

Display Over Other Apps

Used only for the Floating Game Overlay (VIP) feature while playing supported games.

Notification Policy Access

Used only when you enable Focus Mode (Do Not Disturb) to reduce interruptions during gameplay.

VPN Permission

Used only for the Ghost Mode feature. The VPN is created locally on your device to temporarily block selected applications or notifications during gameplay. No browsing history, passwords, or internet traffic are collected or transmitted.

Modify System Settings

Used only when changing screen brightness through the in-game overlay.

Foreground Service

Used to keep Game Mode active while a gaming session is running.

Local Storage

Clean Game Turbo stores settings only on your device, including:

  • Selected Games
  • Game Settings
  • Floating Overlay Settings
  • Focus Mode Preferences
  • Theme Preferences
  • VIP Status
  • Application Preferences

This information never leaves your device.

Advertisements

The free version may display advertisements provided by Google AdMob. VIP users do not see advertisements.

In-App Purchases

VIP subscriptions are securely processed by Google Play Billing. We never receive or store your payment information.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Play Services
  • Google Play Billing
  • Google AdMob

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Clean Game Turbo is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available within the application.
