Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

Clean Game Turbo ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application uses permissions, what information is accessed, and how your information is protected.

Information We Access

Clean Game Turbo may access the following information only to provide its features:

  • Installed games for displaying and launching supported games.
  • Storage information to analyze device storage usage.
  • Photos, videos and audio files only after permission is granted.
  • Notification access if Focus Mode is enabled.
  • Usage Access permission if game monitoring features are enabled.
  • Display over other apps if Floating Game Overlay is enabled.
  • System settings if Brightness Boost or related features are enabled.

We do not collect personal information such as your name, contacts, passwords, messages, or photos for advertising purposes.

Permissions

The application may request:

  • Notification Permission
  • Photos, Videos and Audio Permission
  • Usage Access Permission
  • Display Over Other Apps Permission
  • Modify System Settings Permission

Permissions are requested only when required for the corresponding feature.

Advertising

The free version of Clean Game Turbo displays advertisements using Google AdMob.

Google may collect anonymous information according to its own Privacy Policy.

Google Privacy Policy

VIP users enjoy an ad-free experience.

In-App Purchases

Clean Game Turbo offers optional VIP subscriptions through Google Play Billing. Payments are securely processed by Google. We never receive or store your payment information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. Anonymous technical information may be processed by Google services to provide advertising and payment functionality.

Data Security

Most storage analysis is performed locally on your device. We take reasonable measures to protect your information.

Children's Privacy

Clean Game Turbo is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
