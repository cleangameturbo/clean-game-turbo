Clean Game Turbo

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2026

Clean Game Turbo ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and how the application protects your data.

Information We Collect

Clean Game Turbo does not require users to create an account.

The application may access device information required to provide Game Booster features, including:

  • Installed applications selected by the user
  • Storage statistics
  • Media file information
  • Battery information
  • Memory usage
  • CPU usage
  • Network information

All processing is performed locally on your device unless otherwise stated.

Advertising

The free version of Clean Game Turbo displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob.

Google may collect certain information to provide personalized or non-personalized ads according to its own Privacy Policy.

VIP users do not receive advertisements.

In-App Purchases

VIP subscriptions and purchases are securely processed by Google Play Billing.

We do not receive or store your payment information.

Accessibility Service

Accessibility Service is used only for the optional Auto Mode feature available to VIP users.

When enabled, Accessibility Service detects when a selected game starts and automatically applies the game settings previously configured by the user.

Accessibility Service is NOT used to:

  • Read personal messages
  • Collect passwords
  • Monitor personal activities
  • Collect personal information

VPN Service

The optional Ghost Mode feature uses Android VPN Service to temporarily block network traffic from selected messaging applications while gaming.

No VPN traffic is transmitted to external servers.

The VPN operates locally on your device only.

Notification Access

If enabled by the user, Notification Access is used only to temporarily reduce notification interruptions while gaming.

Notifications are not uploaded or stored on external servers.

Overlay Permission

Overlay permission is used only to display optional performance information such as RAM usage, CPU usage, and device temperature while playing games.

Storage Access

Storage permissions are used only to analyze storage usage and help users identify photos, videos, audio files, cache files, and temporary files.

Your personal files never leave your device.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

Children's Privacy

Clean Game Turbo is not intended for children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Any changes will be published on this page.
